Experimenting with AI making us smarter

Current AI models are smarted than us in some ways (breadth of knowledge, cognitive speed, not being affected by emotions / status games) and dumber than us in other ways (basic arithmetic, semantic understanding of what things really mean).

I am fascinated with how we can leverage AI to make us humans smarter and better able to make decisions together.

I have built a couple features to explore this potential:

  • AI-powered email aliases so that multiple people can interact with the same AI through email.
  • Hierarchical book summarization that uses AI to make it easier to find the parts of books you find interesting.

This is a work in progress, so please reach out if you have any comments.
